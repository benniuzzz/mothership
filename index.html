    Afterjuly exclusive sale up to 80% off in collaboration with Singa Polah

    Exclusively on Mothership only!

    Siti Hawa | Sponsored | August 15, 2020, 10:00 AM

    Events

    The City of Good Show

    24 June 2020 - 12 August 2020

    Giving SG's Facebook page

    AfterJuly is on sale next weekend! Be sure to check out their online store for attractive deals on all dresses with discounts of up to 80% off. The sale will take place from Aug 22 to 23,2020 so don't miss out on this opportunity.

    Look forward to a variety of fashion that you can browse from the comfort of your own home, including dresses, tops, pants, shorts and skirts. Stocks will be limited so you will want to be quick on your fingers.

    Singa Polah

    This sale is in collaboration with Singa Polah, the popular Singaporean lion character, known for their sticker packs in several instant messenging apps like Whatsapp and Telegram.

    In conjunction with the sale, AfterJuly will be releasing exclusive new dresses, including Polah the Lion and the Signficant Otter themed ones. So, fans of Singa Polah can look forward to these unique designs, starting next week.

    The founder of AfterJuly is personally also a huge fan of Singa Polah, and explained that she has always wanted to collaborate with Singa Polah. Thus, this is also a dream come true for her.

    Story Behind

    The creator of Singa Polah gave us an exclusive look into how they came up with the Polah and the Otter:

    The two characters were actually inspired by a real life pet owner and his relationship with his pet. It all started when the owner found his pet in 2018 through an app that matches owners to potential pets.

    OwnerMeetsPet

    After starting to chat and banter on the app, the owner and pet hit it off. They chatted late into the night, talking about everything and nothing. Until one day, the owner decided to ask the pet out to meet for the first time.

    Much to the pet's surprise, the owner suggested going to the karaoke for their first meeting. They were both very anxious but were filled with anticipation.

    First Date(?)

    The owner was not sure it was a first date or not, fearing that he would frighten the pet or make her uncomfortable. Although he behaved awkwardly and shy at first, they started to be more comfortable with each other. They had a wonderful time in the karaoke, and dinner afterwards.

    They continued to meet up subsequently, including watching a movie together and taking a stroll down Singapore River while enjoying their favorite activity of owner-pet conversations.

    As they met up more and grew closer, their feelings for each other grew stronger too. They started to have many firsts in this period, like the first time the owner sent the pet home, and the first time they met each others' families.

    Confession

    The owner decided to confess his feelings to the pet on her birthday. He wrote a card and hid it (abit too well) in the wrapped birthday present for the pet. The pet managed to find the card in the end and read it. Although the pet didnt give the owner an answer straight away, they managed to have the "talk" a few days later.

    Complications appeared at the same time when the owner had a health scare that landed him in A&E. He let the pet worry for him but also was touched by how much she cared for him. The pet took care of the owner and accepted his health complication too.

    Just one month after the confession, the owner officially adopted the pet as his own. It was a new beginning for the both of them as they embarked on a journey together.

    Paw in Hand

    In the time ahead, they went through many challenges together but not a single regret. There were ups and downs, both joy and sorrow, but all shared.

    They had their first overseas trip together in taiwan.

    They served in church and made new friends together.

    They attended concerts together.

    They cooked and baked together.

    They searched for a new home together.

    But there was still one thing missing... the pet still didnt have a pet collar. So the owner decided after two years, that it was time to pop the question to her, and promote his petie into his wifie.

    What will her answer be?
